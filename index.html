**__Pitches:__**
**1. The Blue Rose**
> *Showbiz has taken the coastal area of Rephraim by storm, and none better than The Newcomers, the multi-day dating competition! But something is afoot, and as members of the Rogue's Guild, you're going to find out what it is - no matter the cost.*
> A scenario for Dungeons and Dragons. Prep: 5th-level characters.
**2. Let's See Paul Allen's Character Sheet**
> *Centuries into the future, Intraea still holds adventure for those who seek it. The new frontier: the office. The treasure: a promotion.*
> A scenario for Dungeons and Dragons. Prep: 5th-level characters.
**3. The Carrow Cookbook**
> *The Carrow Cookbook is not a real cookbook. It is a dice-based dish-building game you can play with your friends. In this game, you and your friends have somehow found your way into the Towers Carrow, a mysterious hotel in Virginia. You are informed that you must make a dish for one Lucius Carrow, or face the consequences: being eaten alive.*
> A scenario for The Carrow Cookbook. Prep: none.
**4. Pyramidic Archaeosophy**
> *Think about your favorite Blue Lions from the hit game Fire Emblem: Three Houses. Now strip them of the game’s context and put them in the modern era as childhood friends and cryptozoology hobbyists and throw them into a horror comedy.*
> A scenario for A Game in Which the Faerghus Four Hunt a Monster. Prep: characters.
