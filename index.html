**1. Milky Way Mongooses**
> *Life on the run in the Milky Way isn't easy. And with the tab you've racked up at Julia's bar, she isn't planning on making it any easier. To clear your tab, you've been offered a job you're in no position to refuse: steal the rare cocktail ingredient A-N41 from the infamous bootlegging duo known as the Two-Headed Snake. *
> A scenario for Milky Way Mongooses (a game I made myself). Prep: space furries.
**2. GLHF**
> *You've died and gone to Hell. Had you been less fortunate, you might've gotten sent to some other circle of Hell, but here in the seventh, we like to have fun. You have a chance to leave! - provided you do well in the beloved pastime sport of Hell, Scrubship. *
> A scenario for ISPL (a game I made myself). Prep: characters.
**3. The Blancmange and Thistle**
> *The Blancmange and Thistle is an extravagant hotel cast in gold and chrome. Chandeliers, ironwork, thick colorful mismatched carpets, paintings of every style, taste, and era, guests in nooks buried in deep chairs seemingly built for them, mandrills everywhere, the smell of brass polish and artificial cherry in the air. Your objective: to get to your rooms at the sixth floor. The catch: the unbearable decorum of the other hotel guests.*
> A scenario for Troika! Prep: characters.
**4. So You've Been Thrown Down a Well**
> *You and your unlucky fellows have been, justifiably, thrown down the Well. You acknowledge your infraction and regret being caught, at the very least. Unbound, you have each been thrown. One after the other. Head(s) first. Down the well.*
> A scenario for Troika! Prep: characters.
